# Sentinel Treasury — Pitch

## Project
**Sentinel Treasury**
*The AI copilot for compliant on-chain treasury management*

## One-liner
AI treasury copilot that decides how much capital to keep liquid, how much to deploy for yield, and how much to reserve for payments — with explainable and policy-gated execution on HashKey Chain.

## 45-Second Pitch

**Problem:** DAOs, funds, and on-chain teams hold idle capital earning nothing, with no risk visibility and no compliance layer. Every allocation decision is manual, slow, and opaque.

**Solution:** Sentinel Treasury is an AI copilot that watches your treasury 24/7. It detects idle capital, recommends yield routes, flags risks, and reserves liquidity for payouts — with explainable reasoning.

**How it works:** Deposit funds. AI analyzes balances, oracle feeds, and vault performance. It recommends actions like "move 40% to yield, keep 60% as reserve." An authorized approver confirms on-chain. Every decision is logged with a full explanation of why.

**Why HashKey Chain:** Native KYC SBT for identity-gated approvals, Safe-compatible policy control, and on-chain oracles for real-time data.

**Positioning:** Sentinel Treasury brings AI, compliance-aware approvals, and programmable capital allocation into one treasury operating layer for HashKey Chain.

**Vision:** The operating system for compliant on-chain capital.
